# Wikidata Entity Strategy for Knowledge Graph Optimization

This guide outlines the steps to establish your brand entity on Wikidata and claim your Google Knowledge Panel.

## Step 1: Create a Wikidata Entity

### Prerequisites
- Create a Wikidata account at [wikidata.org](https://www.wikidata.org)
- Gather verifiable sources about your company (press mentions, official registrations)

### Creating the Entity

1. Go to [Create a new item](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Special:NewItem)
2. Fill in:
   - **Label (en)**: IPTV Provider
   - **Description (en)**: IPTV streaming service provider offering live TV channels worldwide
   - **Aliases**: IPTVProvider.me, Best IPTV Provider

### Essential Properties to Add

| Property | ID | Value |
|----------|-----|-------|
| Instance of | P31 | Q4830453 (business) |
| Official website | P856 | https://www.iptvprovider.me |
| Country | P17 | (your registered country) |
| Inception | P571 | 2020 |
| Industry | P452 | Q1060952 (streaming media) |
| Facebook ID | P2013 | iptvprovider |
| X username | P2002 | iptvprovider |
| Instagram username | P2003 | iptvprovider |
| YouTube channel ID | P2397 | @iptvprovider |
| LinkedIn company ID | P4264 | iptvprovider |
| Email address | P968 | support@iptvprovider.me |

## Step 2: Claim Your Google Knowledge Panel

### Eligibility Check
1. Search "IPTV Provider" on Google
2. Look for a Knowledge Panel on the right side
3. If present, find "Claim this knowledge panel" link at bottom

### Verification Process
1. Click "Claim this knowledge panel"
2. Sign in with a Google account
3. Verify ownership via:
   - Google Search Console (recommended)
   - Official social media accounts
   - YouTube channel verification
   
### If No Panel Exists Yet
Continue building authority through:
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across web
- Press mentions and backlinks
- Active social media presence
- Customer reviews on Google Business Profile

---

## Step 3: Maintenance

### Monthly Tasks
- [ ] Verify Wikidata information is current
- [ ] Check for vandalism on your entity
- [ ] Update any changed social links
- [ ] Monitor Knowledge Panel for accuracy

### Best Practices
- Always cite reliable sources when editing Wikidata
- Keep information consistent across all platforms
- Respond to suggested edits on Knowledge Panel
- Report inaccuracies through Google's feedback form
